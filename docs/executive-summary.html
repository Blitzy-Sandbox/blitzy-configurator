Risks & Mitigation

Risk Impact Mitigation
Cross-layer coordination gaps Schedule slippage between teams depends-on: links enforced on every cross-layer story
Observability omitted at launch Blind operations, slow incident response EP-011 delivers logs, traces, metrics, and health checks
