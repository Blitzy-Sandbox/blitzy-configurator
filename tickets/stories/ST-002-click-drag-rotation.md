## Narrative

As an end user, I want to rotate the ball by clicking and dragging it, so that I can inspect every region of the design.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [x] Pressing and dragging the primary pointer inside the preview area rotates the ball in the direction of the drag.
- [x] Rotation continues to follow the pointer for the duration of the drag with no perceptible input lag on the reference hardware profile.
- [x] Releasing the pointer leaves the ball at its final rotated orientation without snapping back to a prior position.
- [x] The ball can be rotated freely about any axis across the full range of motion, with no unreachable viewing angles.
