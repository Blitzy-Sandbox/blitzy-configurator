## Narrative

As an end user, I want to position my uploaded logo on a chosen panel, so that the branding lands exactly where I want it on the ball.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] The uploaded logo can be dragged across the surface of a selected panel on the preview and remains at its new position when released.
- [ ] Coordinate input fields accept numeric entry for the logo's position, and the preview reflects the entered coordinates within the documented latency budget.
- [ ] The logo position is clamped to the boundaries of the selected panel so the logo cannot be placed outside the panel area.
- [ ] The current logo position is displayed in a human-readable form in the control sidebar and updates as the user moves the logo.
